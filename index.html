Welcome To Carriglen

Carriglen Guest House is a private yet tranquil house nestled in a small picturesque village of Ashford County Wicklow. Carriglen is a bright fresh airy country house. Whether its horse riding, golfing, water sports, walking or just a short break, Carriglen offers something for everyone. Accommodation consists of 3 large ensuite bedrooms. Carriglen Bed and Breakfast is one of the most beautiful properties offering luxury accommodation in Wicklow. Each Bedroom is spacious, recently decorated to a high standard. We offer 3 large ensuite bedrooms which are bright and comfortable. The guests have three reception rooms to choose from, reading room, living room with T.V and a sun room which they can enjoy a lovely home cooked meal. Carriglen Guest House offers a comfortable affordable accommodation to suit any type of holiday maker. Many of our guests come to the Carriglen Bed & Breakfast time and time again as they get first class service, enjoy the food and the hospitality!
